Welcome to Milliways!

Milliways rattery is a new rattery based in Southampton, Hampshire. My name is Angela, and I've been keeping rats for 3 years. I had a couple as a child, but have only got back into keeping these wonderful creatures recently. They are very much a large part of my life, and I don't know what I'd do without them!

I aim to breed happy, healthy rats suitable both for pet homes and showing. The variety I will be working on is Russian blue point Siamese (both red and black-eyed), as well as seal point Siamese. There will probably also be blacks, Russian blues with the odd agouti and dumbo.
